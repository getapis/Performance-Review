ROUND 02

Visibility & Accountability.

A clearer daily rhythm for priorities, operations, exceptions, reporting, and decisions.

Before

Operational information was spread across Clubworx, inboxes, spreadsheets, reports, and individual team knowledge.

What I improved

I established consistent ownership of daily administration and introduced clearer start-of-day, end-of-day, weekly reporting, and exception-flagging routines.

Impact

Melissa gained faster visibility into priorities, completed work, blockers, members needing attention, and the health of recurring operations.

  • Daily Clubworx analysis, GetFit inbox management, and operational task handling.
  • Reviewing flags, waivers, suspensions, cancellations, failed payments, and expiring memberships.
  • Maintaining the New Member Spreadsheet and Daily Numbers Spreadsheet.
  • Updating attendance, rollover, and fringe-reach-out information.
  • Supporting Red Flag and Orange Flag member outreach.
  • Preparing weekly numbers, attendance links, and low-attendance callouts.
  • Checking agreements, debit dates, rollover status, and membership exceptions.
  • Supporting invoicing, MYOB, the Xero transition, and management-report setup.
  • Daily start-of-day / end-of-day updates on priorities, progress, and blockers.
Improvement: The work moved from task-by-task support to a visible operating rhythm with clearer ownership and fewer hidden exceptions.
